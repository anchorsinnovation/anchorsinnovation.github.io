About Us


ANCHORS Innovation Limited, it stands for Analytical Network for Construction Hazards and Operational Risks Solutions.

Our team has been involved in award winning smart building projects, in which similar BIM and IoT solutions have been used.

With a long track record from the industry and academic, our team focuses on developing technology for industry adoption.
